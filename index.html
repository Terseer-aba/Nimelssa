Who we are

We are aspiring Medical Laboratory Scientist, a profession sadled with the responsibility of carrying out diagnosis or investigation on human specimen to reveal his health status.

  • Our Mission

    To be the leading voice and advocate for Nigerian medical laboratory science students, fostering excellence in education, research, and professional development, while contributing to the advancement of healthcare in Nigeria.p>

  • Our Vision

    The mission of the Nigerian Medical Laboratory Science Students' Association (NIMELSSA) is to empower and support students pursuing medical laboratory science education in Nigeria.

  • Our Value

    integrity, Reliability, Precision, Accuracy and Confidentiality
