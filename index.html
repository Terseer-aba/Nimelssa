Who we are

We are aspiring Medical Laboratory Scientist, a profession sadled with the responsibility of carrying out diagnosis or investigation on human specimen to reveal his health status.

  • Our Mission

    To be the leading voice and advocate for Nigerian medical laboratory science students, fostering excellence in education, research, and professional development, while contributing to the advancement of healthcare in Nigeria.

    Academic excellence

    NIMELSSA focuse on supporting its members' academic pursuits by organizing study groups, workshop, and seminars. These activities aims at enhancing students understanding of medical laboratory science and keep them up to date with the latest development in the field.

    Profesional development

    NIMELSSA encourage student to participate in conferences, both National and international to broarden their horizon and expose them to various aspects of medical laboratory science. This exposure helped students connect with professionals in the field and provide valuable networking opportunities.

    Research and Innovation

    The Association promote research initiatives among its members, fostering a culture of scientific inquiring and innovation within the medical laborary science students community. This enphasis on research aimed to contribute to the advancement of medical laboratory science in Nigeria.

    Community outreach

    NIMELSSA members recognized the importance of giving back to the society. They organize blood donation drives, free testing and awareness programs on various health issues, making positive contributions to the local community.

  • Our Vision

    The mission of the Nigerian Medical Laboratory Science Students' Association (NIMELSSA) is to empower and support students pursuing medical laboratory science education in Nigeria.

  • Our Value

    integrity, Reliability, Precision, Accuracy and Confidentiality

  • Our History

    The Nigeria medical laborary science Students' Association (NIMELSSA) was founded in 1975 to bring together students pursuing medical laboratory science as their choosing field of tudy in Nigeria. This professional organization sought to promote academic excellence, research, and networking among future medical laborary scientist in the country. The Association was established during a crucial time when the need for a unified platform for medical laborary science became apparent.

    Over the years, NIMELSSA continues to grow in both size and influence. it became an essential part of the Nigerian medical education landscape, with its members actively participating in shaping the future of medical laborary science in the country. The association's leadership and members work in collaboration with other medical and health related organisation to address issues related to healthcare, researchand policy implementation.
